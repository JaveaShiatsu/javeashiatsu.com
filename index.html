Philosophy

Shiatsu is more than touch — it is a dialogue between practitioner and body, a listening that reaches beneath the surface to where energy gathers, stagnates, or calls for release.

Rooted in the Zen tradition, this practice honours stillness as much as movement, presence as much as technique.

About

A practice built on presence

I discovered Zen Shiatsu through my own journey of healing and have dedicated years to deepening this practice — studying its roots in Traditional Chinese Medicine, its expression through the Zen lineage, and its application to the challenges of modern life.

Each session is shaped entirely around you. I listen to what your body communicates before a single word is spoken, and work with — never against — the intelligence it already holds.

Based in beautiful Javea on the Costa Blanca, I welcome clients seeking relief from physical tension, emotional stress, or simply the desire to feel more at home in themselves.

Ways I can help

Each offering is designed to meet you where you are — whether you are experiencing acute discomfort, managing ongoing stress, or simply wish to maintain your wellbeing through regular practice.

Shiatsu can be beneficial for a wide range of conditions, including headaches and migraines, neck and shoulder tension, lower back pain, sciatica, digestive disorders, insomnia, fatigue, anxiety, depression, menstrual complaints, circulatory problems, and rheumatic or arthritic conditions. It is equally valuable as preventive care — supporting those in good health who simply wish to feel more vital, balanced, and at ease in daily life.
